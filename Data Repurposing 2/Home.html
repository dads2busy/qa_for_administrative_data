Data Structure

Is the data appropriately structured for purposes of analysis?

Because datasets are often created for reasons of administration and reporting, and further that there are generally few constraints on their organization, administrative datasets are often constructed in manners not conducive to statistical analyses. Accordingly, many times there are issues of data structure that must be identified and resolved.
